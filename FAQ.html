Frequently Asked Questions

What is food insecurity?

Food insecurity is a sweeping global problem in which people don't have access to a sufficient amount of quality food. Factors that could lead to food insecurity are poverty, health conditions, and systematic racism. Typically, food insecurity affects minority groups like black and indigenous people are affected by food insecurity the most; however, children and the elderly are susceptible to it as well.

Who are we?

We are a group of like minded individuals who strive to make the world a better place. You can read more about our team on the About Us page on this site.

What are the consequences of food insecurity?

Food insecurity and the lack of access to affordable nutritious food can result in chronic illnesses like asthma as well as behavioral problems like anxiety. Additionally, food insecurity can cause delayed development in children.

How can I get involved?

There are many ways you can help aid in the fight against food insecurity. Through our site, you can donate money or find locations where you can donate food for families battling hunger. You can also sign up to volunteer in your community to help distribute food to those who need it.
